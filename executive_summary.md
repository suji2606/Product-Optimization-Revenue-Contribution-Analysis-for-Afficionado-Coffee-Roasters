# Executive Summary
## Afficionado Coffee Roasters — Product Optimization Analysis (2025)

**Scope:** 149,116 transactions | 3 NYC Stores | 80 Products | Total Revenue: $698,812

---

## Situation
Afficionado Coffee Roasters operates 80 products across 3 locations without structured visibility into which products drive revenue and which underperform. This analysis provides data-driven intelligence to support menu optimisation.

---

## Key Findings

### Revenue Performance
- **Total revenue: $698,812** | Coffee (38.6%) + Tea (28.1%) = **66.7% of all revenue**
- **Drinking Chocolate** leads efficiency at **$18,104 revenue per SKU** (only 4 products)
- All 3 stores perform almost identically: Hell's Kitchen $236K · Astoria $232K · Lower Manhattan $230K

### Hero Products (Top 3)
1. Sustainably Grown Organic Lg — **$21,152** (Drinking Chocolate)
2. Dark Chocolate Lg — **$21,006** (Drinking Chocolate)
3. Latte Rg — **$19,112** (Coffee)

### Underperformers
- **7 of bottom 10 products are Loose Tea** — each earning under $1,600/year
- Loose Tea category: 8 SKUs averaging just **$1,402 each** (worst efficiency)
- Weakest product: Dark Chocolate packaged — **$755** (0.11% of revenue)

### Menu Concentration
- **42 products → 80% of revenue** (Pareto rule confirmed)
- Simplifying to ~55 SKUs preserves ~95%+ of revenue while cutting complexity

---

## Recommendations

| Priority | Action | Expected Outcome |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| 🔴 Immediate | Loose Tea: 8 → 3 SKUs | Simpler menu, faster service |
| 🔴 Immediate | Amplify Drinking Chocolate in marketing & placement | Higher-margin revenue growth |
| 🟡 Short-term | Remove bottom 15 SKUs (pilot trial) | Operational efficiency |
| 🟡 Short-term | Coffee Beans: 10 → 5 premium SKUs | Better retail positioning |
| 🟢 Ongoing | Centralise menu decisions across all 3 stores | Consistent customer experience |

---

## Financial Opportunity
Redirecting demand from low-impact to hero products could increase average transaction value by **5–8%**, translating to an additional **$35,000–$56,000 per year** without adding any new products.
